Introducing here.

here. Where creativity meets understanding, and every mug is crafted to fuel what's next. A third space, not home, not the office but something in-between. A space that brings work, innovation and the perfect brew together. Where big ideas are born. It happens here.

Vision

To establish a seamless, physical "in-between" environment where the local communtiy can naturally transition between stuctured work and casual socializing.

Our space

we said a comfortable and creative spot away from home or the office and we meant it. fitted with sit up chairs for if you are feeling more productive and want to get into your grind and comfy couches that are perfect for relaxation and a laid back feel. don't forget to accompany that with your favourite drink and pastry of choice to get you into your creative mood. all catered to make you want to stay and potentially become part of the here. family.
